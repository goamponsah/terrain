Frequently asked questions
Do I need a credit card to start the free trial?
No. You can start your 90-day free trial with just an email address. No payment details required until you choose to subscribe.
What happens after the 90-day trial?
Your data stays safe and your account remains accessible. You'll receive an email before the trial ends with your founding lodge pricing. There's no obligation to subscribe.
Can I change plans later?
Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing period.
What currencies do you accept?
All subscriptions are billed in USD. Your lodge owners pay in USD regardless of their location — Terrain works for lodges in Kenya, Tanzania, South Africa, Botswana, Zimbabwe and beyond.
Is my lodge data private and secure?
Yes. Each lodge account is completely private. Your pricing data, packages, and revenue figures are never shared with other operators or third parties. Data is stored securely and encrypted.
Who is behind Terrain?
Terrain is built and operated by TechSoftNexa LTD, a technology company registered in Ghana. We build revenue intelligence tools for hospitality and experience operators globally.
